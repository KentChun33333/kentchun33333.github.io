Buy Our First Condo in Singapore

2021 - 6 - 28
| Life Design
| Reading

In 2021 May, my wife and I become PR in Singapore. We then decide to buy a house here for a more stable life.

After considerations, we bought The Woodleight Residence. The location is near the cental of Singapore, Google Map . There is also a 3D Demo for take a virtual look of it.

Why Woodleight Residence?

First, the general critials for us are listed below.

Item Description
Locations The distance to key facilities like vivo-city, stadium and orchard. The surrounding should be good. Less than 5 mins to MRT
Project A good project that is on top of the MRT, which is more important to be freehold vs 99-lease hold. The project size also important that 665 units are a decent number. We also want new house that it should top from 2018 onward.
